Welcome to LCR!

This game has three dice that are rolled to decide where chips end up!
If you roll an L or R you give chips to the player to your left or right.
If you roll a C you give chips to the bank and they leave the field of play.
Finally, if you roll a '*' you keep your chips! Lucky you!.
The winner is the last player to have chips. Good luck and have fun!

To play you need 3 or more players.
